Discover styles that celebrate your unique body shape

Hourglass

Characterized by balanced bust and hips with a well-defined waist.

Styling Tips

  • Emphasize your waist with belts
  • Choose wrap dresses and fitted tops
  • High-waisted bottoms work well

Pear

Wider hips and thighs compared to the upper body with a defined waist.

Styling Tips

  • Draw attention to your upper body
  • A-line skirts balance proportions
  • Dark-colored bottoms minimize hips

Apple

Wider torso and bust with slimmer hips and legs.

Styling Tips

  • Empire waist dresses flatter your shape
  • V-necklines elongate the upper body
  • Flowy tops create a slimming effect

Rectangle

Similar measurements for bust, waist, and hips with minimal curves.

Styling Tips

  • Create curves with peplum tops
  • Belted dresses define your waist
  • Asymmetrical details add dimension

Inverted Triangle

Broader shoulders and bust compared to hips and waist.

Styling Tips

  • Balance with wide-leg pants
  • Scoop necklines soften shoulders
  • Detailing on the lower body draws eye down

Triangle

Wider hips than shoulders, often with a well-defined waist and narrower upper body.

Styling Tips

  • Add volume to your upper body with ruffles or puff sleeves
  • Highlight your shoulders with boat neck or off-shoulder tops
  • Go for A-line skirts or dresses to balance your silhouette

Diamond

Wider midsection with narrower bust and hips.

Styling Tips

  • Wrap dresses create an hourglass illusion
  • Vertical stripes elongate your torso
  • Structured jackets define your waist
